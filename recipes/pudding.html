Vanilla Pudding

Simple, smooth and sweet

This classic vanilla pudding is silky, smooth, and full of rich vanilla flavor. Serve chilled for a perfect dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually stir in milk.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
  3. Boil for 1 minute, then remove from heat. Stir in butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour into serving dishes and chill before serving.
