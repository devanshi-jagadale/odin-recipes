French Fries

Golden, crispy and delicious

These homemade French fries are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. A perfect side for burgers or any comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain and dry completely with a towel.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Fry potatoes in batches for 3–5 minutes until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Season with salt and serve hot.
