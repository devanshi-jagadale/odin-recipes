Homemade Pizza

Customize with your favorite toppings

Homemade pizza is fun to make and even more fun to eat. This simple dough and basic sauce provide a perfect base for any toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  2. Spread sauce evenly over dough. Top with cheese and desired toppings.
  3. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  4. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
