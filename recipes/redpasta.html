Classic Tomato Pasta

A quick and tasty classic

This classic tomato pasta is simple, quick to make, and full of flavor. Made with fresh garlic, onions, crushed tomatoes, and herbs, it's a perfect weekday meal that tastes like it's from a restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil, chopped (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, oregano, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in chopped basil. Serve sauce over pasta. Top with Parmesan if desired.
